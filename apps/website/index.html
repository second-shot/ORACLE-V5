How It Works

The mission loop

Every piece of work moves through the same five movements. No exceptions. No silent drift.

01
Observe
Read Oracle docs before acting. Gather what exists before proposing what should.
02
Map
Separate facts from gaps, risks from opportunities. No movement before the terrain is clear.
03
Decide or Escalate
If within authority and reversible: act. If structural or unclear: escalate before acting.
04
Execute
Perform the work. Clear output. Clear definition of done.
05
Record
Update memory, backlog, and standards. Work that isn't recorded doesn't exist.
